# Blip

**Look:** marker
**Aliases:** ink, drop, blob

A small, deadpan ink-drop creature: a rounded black teardrop body with a clean white
outline, two round white dot eyes and a tiny flat mouth, two short curved stick arms,
and exactly one accent — a small red dot floating just above its head like a spark of an
idea. Keep the silhouette simple and that single red dot present in every image.
